After they were rescued’ from a luxury hotel in Haryana’s Manesar, the Congress MLAs from Madhya Pradesh have been brought to the chief minister’s residence in Bhopal. Eyewitness accounts put the heavy deployment of security personnel outside the CM’s residence in Shymala Hills area of MP’s capital city, Bhopal. Barricading has also been put into place on all routes leading towards the CM residence. Meanwhile, the missing MLAs from the Madhya Pradesh Assembly have been traced to villas near Whitefield, the tech corridor of Bengaluru city in Karnataka. It is being reported that the legislators have been living in these villas since Wednesday afternoon. Their cell phones are also switched off.
